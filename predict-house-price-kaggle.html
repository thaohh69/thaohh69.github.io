Multivariables Analysis

There are two types of feature in housing data : categorical and numerical

In terms of categorical data, it is like labels and follows some kind of pattern. For example, in this study we have feature “Downtown” and it consists of other categorical responses – “Yes”, “No”, “Near”, “Far”. The price of house then could be affected by these features ( high or low)

Numerical data is in form of number which could follow linear regression theory. For example, the square of area could be in relationship with housing price. Larger the house is, more expensive the cost of it is!
